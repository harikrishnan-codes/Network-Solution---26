Common Questions

Everything you need to know about the NetSolve fiber experience.

What makes fiber different from cable internet?

Fiber uses light pulses through glass strands, offering symmetric speeds (same upload/download) and significantly lower latency compared to traditional copper cable networks.

How long does the professional installation take?

Our expert engineers typically complete a full residential installation in 60 to 90 minutes, including specialized router configuration and signal testing.

Is there really no data cap on the 1Gbps plan?

Correct. We believe in true connectivity. There are zero data caps, no throttling, and no "fair usage" policies that slow you down.

Can I use my own mesh Wi-Fi system?

Yes. While we provide a Wi-Fi 6 gateway, our system is fully compatible with popular mesh networks like Eero, Google Nest, and Orbi via bridge mode.
